Semester Examination System

About Semester System

The college follows a semester-based examination system instead of the traditional annual system. The semester breaks into two main terms per academic year, ensuring continuous engagement of students and faculty throughout the year. This approach reduces exam burden and promotes more continuous evaluation, leading to better learning outcomes and reduced academic stress.

SGPA - Semester Grade Point Average

SGPA is the weighted average of grade points obtained in all courses registered by a student in a semester. It provides a measure of the student's performance in that particular semester.

SGPA = Σ(Credit × Grade Point) / Σ(Credits)

CGPA - Cumulative Grade Point Average

CGPA is the weighted average of grade points obtained in all courses registered by a student from the first semester onwards. It reflects the overall academic performance throughout the program.

CGPA = Σ(Credit × Grade Point) / Σ(Total Credits)
